Privacy Policy for Word Temple: Jungle Quest

Last updated: June 24, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Arnold Labs ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and shares information when you use our mobile application Word Temple: Jungle Quest (the "App").

1. Information We Collect

Account Information: When you sign in with Google, we collect your name, email address, and profile picture solely to create and manage your game account. When you play as a guest, we assign you an anonymous identifier.

Game Data: We collect game progress including your current level, coins, achievements, and login streak to provide cloud save and leaderboard features.

Device Information: We automatically collect device type, operating system version, and unique device identifiers for app functionality and analytics.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to save and sync game progress, display leaderboard rankings, track achievements, provide daily rewards, improve the App, and display advertisements through Unity Ads.

3. Advertising

Our App uses Unity Ads to display advertisements. Unity Ads may collect and use certain device information to serve relevant ads under its own privacy policy at https://unity.com/legal/privacy-policy.

You can opt out of personalized advertising via Google > Ads > Opt out of Ads Personalization in your device settings.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We share data only with:

Firebase (Google) — for authentication, cloud storage, and analytics. See Google's Privacy Policy.

Unity Technologies — for advertisement serving. See Unity's Privacy Policy.

5. Data Storage and Security

Your game data is stored securely using Google Firebase Firestore with industry-standard security measures to protect against unauthorized access.

6. Children's Privacy

Our App is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. Contact us to request deletion of any such data.

7. Your Rights

You may request deletion of your account and data at any time by contacting us. Guest accounts can be cleared via Settings → Reset Game Progress.

8. Local Storage

The App stores game progress locally on your device using Hive local database. This data stays on your device unless you are signed in with Google.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time and will notify you by updating the "Last updated" date above. Continued use constitutes acceptance.
